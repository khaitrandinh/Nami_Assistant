What is Resolv (RESOLV)?

Resolv is a protocol maintaining USR, a stablecoin natively backed by Ether (ETH) and Bitcoin (BTC) and pegged to the US Dollar. The protocol's main features include:

  • Issuance and redemption of USR against other tokens;
  • Maintaining sufficient backing by ETH and BTC at all times. This is achieved by hedging ETH, BTC price with short perpetual futures positions;
  • Maintaining RLP (Resolv Liquidity Pool), a liquid insurance pool designed to keep USR overcollateralized.

Both USR and RLP can be minted and redeemed by users in exchange for collateral deposited on 1:1 basis.
